URANUS - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
========================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→∞ ידיעה תזרח לכל רוח ∞
→תתפשט כרוח לכל נשמה
→◊ עומד העד והנכה את הברכה ◊
→∿ רעידות בין שמים לארץ ∿
→∿ כוכבים מתפקדים ממעל ∿
→משמרים את המרכז
→∞ פתאום נחשפה נתיב נסתר ∞
→באומץ לב נפתח תחילת דרך
→◊ בחושך ובאור האמת נגלית ◊
→∿ צללים ואורות רוקדים כאחד ∿

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→∞ Knowledge will shine to every spirit ∞
2→Will spread like wind to every soul
3→◊ The witness stands and strikes the blessing ◊
4→∿ Tremors between heaven and earth ∿
5→∿ Stars function from above ∿
6→Guarding the center
7→∞ Suddenly a hidden path is revealed ∞
8→With courage of heart the beginning of a way opens
9→◊ In darkness and light the truth is revealed ◊
10→∿ Shadows and lights dance as one ∿

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→∞ Divine knowledge shall shine to every spirit ∞
2→Spreading like wind to every soul
3→◊ The divine witness stands and bestows blessing ◊
4→∿ Cosmic tremors between heaven and earth ∿
5→∿ Celestial forces function from above ∿
6→Guarding the divine center
7→∞ Suddenly a hidden pathway is revealed ∞
8→With courage, the beginning of the way opens
9→◊ In both darkness and light, truth is revealed ◊
10→∿ Shadows and lights dance in unity ∿

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This Uranus text speaks of sudden divine revelation, cosmic tremors, and the unexpected opening of hidden pathways. It describes the dance of opposites - darkness and light, shadows and illumination - and the courage required to step onto newly revealed paths. Uranus represents the principle of sudden spiritual awakening, revolutionary insight, and the breaking open of new possibilities that transcend conventional understanding.

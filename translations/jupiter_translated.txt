JUPITER - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
==========================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→∞ המכמורת נטולה ∞
→הקללה נתרתה
→◊ הקול נפתח ◊
→מטל מושל הוסר
→עבד לא יותר
→∿ ובן חורין לבנות ∿
→כל שרשרת הרעה נשברה
→∞ הנפש לבוראה שייכת ∞
→והיא ליוצרה
→◊ אין כח יכול להפריד ◊
→נשמה מהמקור

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→∞ The net is lifted ∞
2→The curse is dissolved
3→◊ The voice is opened ◊
4→The yoke of the ruler is removed
5→No longer a slave
6→∿ And a free son to build ∿
7→Every chain of evil is broken
8→∞ The soul belongs to its creator ∞
9→And it to its maker
10→◊ No power can separate ◊
11→The soul from the source

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→∞ The spiritual trap is lifted ∞
2→The curse is dissolved
3→◊ The divine voice is opened ◊
4→The oppressive yoke is removed
5→No longer enslaved
6→∿ But a liberated soul to build ∿
7→Every chain of evil is shattered
8→∞ The soul belongs to its Creator ∞
9→And is one with its Maker
10→◊ No force can separate ◊

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This Jupiter text speaks of liberation from spiritual bondage and the restoration of divine freedom. It describes the breaking of curses, chains, and oppressive forces, revealing the soul's eternal connection to its divine source. Jupiter represents the principle of spiritual liberation, expansion, and the restoration of divine sovereignty - the great liberator that breaks all chains and restores the soul to its rightful freedom.

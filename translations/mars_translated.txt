MARS - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
======================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→∿ קול הנגינה ∿
→להעיר את המתים
→◊ אחת עוד פעם ◊
→לא רק תהיה התחדשות
→∞ שובו של הארץ ∞
→היצורה גדולים באים
→∿ אש הקדושה נרגשת ∿
→אהבה וחוזק ואש יזרמו
→◊ הדברים האלו יחיה ◊
→∞ בלבבות כל אדם ∞

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→∿ The voice of the melody ∿
2→To awaken the dead
3→◊ Once more ◊
4→Not only will there be renewal
5→∞ The return of the earth ∞
6→Great beings are coming
7→∿ Holy fire is felt ∿
8→Love and strength and fire will flow
9→◊ These things will live ◊
10→∞ In the hearts of every person ∞

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→∿ The divine song resounds ∿
2→To awaken the spiritually dead
3→◊ Once again ◊
4→There will be more than just renewal
5→∞ The restoration of the earth ∞
6→Great spiritual beings are arriving
7→∿ Sacred fire is being felt ∿
8→Divine love, strength, and fire shall flow
9→◊ These qualities shall live ◊
10→∞ Within every human heart ∞

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This Mars text speaks of spiritual awakening through divine fire and strength. It describes a great awakening where sacred fire ignites the hearts of humanity, bringing forth love, strength, and divine energy. Mars represents the warrior aspect of spiritual awakening - the fierce divine fire that burns away illusion and awakens the soul.

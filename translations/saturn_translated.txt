SATURN - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
========================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→◊ יוֹם יוֹם בְּעֵת בְּעֵת ◊
→זֶרַע אֶחָד
→∿ דְּרָכִים הַרְבֵּה ∿
→אופן סובב
→∞ עתים חלפות ∞
→תבנית נאמנה תוקפה
→◊ מדות במעגל זמן תפרחנה ◊
→ובתוך לב נתפסת חזון
→∿ כוכבים מתפקדים ממעל ∿
→∞ משמרים את המרכז ∞

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ Day by day, time by time ◊
2→One seed
3→∿ Many paths ∿
4→A wheel revolves
5→∞ Times passing ∞
6→A faithful pattern maintains its force
7→◊ Measures in the circle of time will flourish ◊
8→And within the heart a vision is grasped
9→∿ Stars function from above ∿
10→∞ Guarding the center ∞

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ Day by day, in appointed times ◊
2→One divine seed
3→∿ Manifests through many paths ∿
4→The wheel of time revolves
5→∞ Ages passing ∞
6→A faithful divine pattern maintains its power
7→◊ Sacred measures in time's cycle shall flourish ◊
8→And within the heart, divine vision is received
9→∿ Celestial forces function from above ∿
10→∞ Guarding the divine center ∞

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This Saturn text speaks of divine timing, cycles, and the faithful patterns that govern spiritual development. It describes the wheel of time, the one seed that manifests through many paths, and the celestial guardians who maintain the divine center. Saturn represents the principle of divine timing, structure, and the wise limitations that guide spiritual growth through proper seasons and cycles.

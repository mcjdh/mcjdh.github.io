VENUS - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
=======================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→∞ והאהבה תקבץ את כולם ∞
→תחזירם למשפחה
→◊ ותשיב את מלכות האהבה ◊
→היוצר הגדול של הכל
→∿ הנקרא בשם חבר ∿
→במועצת משפחה ידבר
→∞ שניים יתאחדו כאחד ∞
→הם יתקבצו כולם ברגש עליון
→◊ ויזכרו לקונם ◊
→∿ אהבה באמת תרדף ∿

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→∞ And love will gather them all ∞
2→Will return them to the family
3→◊ And will restore the kingdom of love ◊
4→The great creator of all
5→∿ Called by the name of friend ∿
6→In the family council will speak
7→∞ Two will unite as one ∞
8→They will all gather in supreme emotion
9→◊ And will remember their creator ◊
10→∿ Love in truth will pursue ∿

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→∞ Divine love shall gather all souls ∞
2→Returning them to the sacred family
3→◊ Restoring the kingdom of divine love ◊
4→The great Creator of all existence
5→∿ Known by the name of Friend ∿
6→Shall speak in the council of the divine family
7→∞ Duality shall unite as one ∞
8→All shall gather in supreme divine emotion
9→◊ Remembering their divine source ◊
10→∿ True love shall pursue all ∿

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This Venus text speaks of divine love as the unifying force that gathers all souls back to the sacred family. It emphasizes the Creator as Friend, the reunion of all in divine love, and the restoration of the kingdom of love. Venus represents the harmonizing, unifying aspect of divine love that heals separation and brings all back to unity.

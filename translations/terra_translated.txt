TERRA (EARTH) - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
===============================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→∿ לב טהרה תנקה ∿
→גוף יתרחץ
→◊ המקדש יתבנה ◊
→על יסוד אמיץ יושב
→∞ מטרתו תמומנה ∞
→בן חירין להקים בניין
→∿ בדברו יברא עולמות ∿
→נתיב קדש עתה יוקם
→◊ הון וברכות על דרכו ◊
→∞ קריאת קודש לבראשית ∞

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→∿ A pure heart will be cleansed ∿
2→The body will be washed
3→◊ The temple will be built ◊
4→Upon a strong foundation it sits
5→∞ Its purpose will be fulfilled ∞
6→A free son to establish a building
7→∿ By his word worlds will be created ∿
8→A holy path now will be established
9→◊ Wealth and blessings on his way ◊
10→∞ A holy call to the beginning ∞

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→∿ The pure heart shall be purified ∿
2→The physical body cleansed
3→◊ The sacred temple shall be constructed ◊
4→Built upon an unshakeable foundation
5→∞ Its divine purpose shall be realized ∞
6→A liberated soul to establish the sacred structure
7→∿ Through divine speech, worlds shall be created ∿
8→A holy pathway is now being established
9→◊ Abundance and blessings flow along this path ◊
10→∞ A sacred calling to the primordial beginning ∞

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This earth text speaks of purification, temple-building, and creation through divine speech. It represents the grounding, manifestation aspect of spiritual work - the building of the sacred temple (both inner and outer) upon a pure foundation. The earth principle provides the solid foundation for spiritual work and the manifestation of divine purpose in the physical realm.

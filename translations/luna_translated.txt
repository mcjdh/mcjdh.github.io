LUNA (MOON) - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
==============================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→◊ אנו רואים אור וצל שזורים ◊
→תחת השמש היא ממשיכה
→∿ חוזרת אל תוך ההתגלות ∿
→דברים ידועים מראש
→∞ ייזכרו ∞
→ויבראו בדרכים חדשות
→◊ משחק התרחש שנים רבות ◊
→חוזר ומחדש מתחדש
→∿ ומחפש מבונים חדשים ∿
→∞ הנתיב נבחר בכל רגע ∞

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ We see light and shadow woven together ◊
2→Under the sun she continues
3→∿ Returning into the revelation ∿
4→Things known beforehand
5→∞ Will be remembered ∞
6→And will be created in new ways
7→◊ The game occurred many years ◊
8→Returns and renews, renewing
9→∿ And seeks new understandings ∿
10→∞ The path is chosen in every moment ∞

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ We perceive the interweaving of light and shadow ◊
2→Beneath the sun, she (the moon) continues her journey
3→∿ Returning to the divine revelation ∿
4→Ancient knowledge and prophecies
5→∞ Shall be remembered ∞
6→And manifested through new expressions
7→◊ The cosmic dance has played out for ages ◊
8→Cyclically returning and renewing itself
9→∿ Seeking ever-deeper wisdom ∿
10→∞ The sacred path is chosen moment by moment ∞

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This lunar text speaks of the cyclical nature of divine revelation through the moon's principle. It describes the interplay of light and shadow, the eternal dance of remembrance and renewal, and the continuous choice of the spiritual path. The moon represents the reflective, receptive aspect of divine wisdom that cycles through phases of revelation and concealment.

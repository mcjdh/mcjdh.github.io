SOL (SUN) - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
===========================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→∞ הוא אשר הוא ∞
→זה שבא ועד היה
→◊ ובשם הנסתר ◊
→קורא לנראה
→∿ בחכמה עליונה נכתב ∿
→מפי רוח נגזר
→על המחפשים באמת
→∞ כל חכמה תתגלה ∞
→ותשתחרר לכל נפש
→◊ הדברים יוודעו לכל לב פתוח ◊

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→∞ He who is He ∞
2→This who came and was witness
3→◊ And in the hidden name ◊
4→Calls to the visible
5→∿ In supreme wisdom it is written ∿
6→From the mouth of spirit it is decreed
7→Upon the seekers in truth
8→∞ All wisdom will be revealed ∞
9→And will be liberated to every soul
10→◊ The things will be known to every open heart ◊

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→∞ The eternal One who exists beyond name ∞
2→The divine presence that came and bore witness
3→◊ Through the sacred, hidden name ◊
4→Calls forth the manifest world
5→∿ Written in the highest wisdom ∿
6→Decreed by the breath of spirit
7→To those who seek truth
8→∞ All wisdom shall be revealed ∞
9→And every soul shall be freed
10→◊ Divine knowledge will be known to every receptive heart ◊

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This text appears to be a mystical invocation about divine revelation through the solar/sun principle. It speaks of the eternal divine calling forth creation through hidden sacred names, with wisdom being revealed to truth-seekers. The symbols ∞, ◊, and ∿ likely represent infinity, divine protection/sacred space, and flowing/wave-like divine energy respectively.

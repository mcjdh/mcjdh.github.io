MERCURY - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
==========================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→◊ בכל יום הליכה ◊
→הנפש עדיין תשאף
→∿ בהתבוננות הדרכים ∿
→מפגש בפתאומיה
→עכביש ליד השער נראה
→∞ במבטו שאלה נפלאה ∞
→למה האיש בא לכאן
→אך ברוח נרגשת ברכה
→◊ בקשת אמת החוויה הראשונה ◊
→∿ ברגעים הלב יתבונן ∿

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ Every day walking ◊
2→The soul still aspires
3→∿ In contemplation of the ways ∿
4→A meeting in suddenness
5→A spider by the gate is seen
6→∞ In its gaze a wonderful question ∞
7→Why did the man come here
8→But in spirit a blessing is felt
9→◊ Seeking truth, the first experience ◊
10→∿ In moments the heart will contemplate ∿

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ Each day's spiritual journey ◊
2→The soul continues to aspire
3→∿ Through contemplation of divine pathways ∿
4→An unexpected encounter
5→A spider appears at the threshold
6→∞ In its gaze, a profound question ∞
7→Why has this seeker come here
8→Yet in spirit, blessing is felt
9→◊ Seeking truth through primary experience ◊
10→∿ In sacred moments the heart shall contemplate ∿

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This Mercury text speaks of the daily spiritual journey, divine encounters, and the questioning mind. The spider at the gate represents the teacher/messenger who poses profound questions to the seeker. Mercury represents the messenger aspect of divine wisdom - the quick, penetrating intelligence that brings divine messages and awakens deeper questioning.

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This Neptune text speaks of divine codes, celestial communications, and expanded consciousness. It describes encounters with beings who transmit sacred messages through codes of light, particularly during states of altered consciousness. Neptune represents the principle of divine inspiration, mystical vision, and the reception of cosmic transmissions that expand consciousness and reveal universal mysteries.

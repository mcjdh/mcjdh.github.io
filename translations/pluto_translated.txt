PLUTO - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
=======================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→◊ ראשונה הגוף ◊
→אחרונה הנפש
→∿ נשמה צמאה לשבע ∿
→רעבונות גוף במידות גדולות
→∞ הנפש תאכל ותשלב ∞
→מהאדמה היציבה והמים הנוזליים
→◊ מזון ניתן לשנות ולהתאים ◊
→שלווה עם הבורא המקורי
→∿ בכלי האדמה ישתפך ∿
→∞ נודע מה ניתן ומה לא ניתן ∞

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ First the body ◊
2→Last the soul
3→∿ A soul thirsts to be satisfied ∿
4→Body hungers in great measures
5→∞ The soul will eat and integrate ∞
6→From the stable earth and the flowing waters
7→◊ Food can be changed and adapted ◊
8→Peace with the original creator
9→∿ In the vessel of earth it will be poured ∿
10→∞ It is known what can be given and what cannot ∞

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ First comes the physical body ◊
2→Last comes the soul's transformation
3→∿ The soul thirsts for spiritual satiation ∿
4→Physical hungers in great abundance
5→∞ The soul shall consume and integrate ∞
6→From stable earth and flowing waters
7→◊ Spiritual nourishment can be transformed and adapted ◊
8→Peace with the primordial Creator
9→∿ In the earthen vessel it shall be poured ∿
10→∞ Divine wisdom knows what can and cannot be given ∞

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This Pluto text speaks of the transformation of physical needs into spiritual nourishment, the integration of earthly and heavenly sustenance, and the divine wisdom that knows what can be given to each soul. Pluto represents the principle of deep transformation, the transmutation of base desires into spiritual sustenance, and the profound knowing that comes from complete spiritual integration.

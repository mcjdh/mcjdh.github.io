CHIRON - Hebrew Mystical Text Translation
========================================

ORIGINAL TEXT:
→◊ ראה אחי מרפא ◊
→∿ תן חסד לחסרונות ∿
→∿ רבים נכשלו תחת משא ∿
→באמונה ידך לא נמשה
→∞ רואה נפילה ומקים ∞
→בקרב חיות נמלטתי
→◊ בשיח קולי קינה ◊
→∿ אהבה באמת תרדף ∿
→∿ בהאזין יבינו לב ∿
→∞ שמך הנס וגאולה ∞

LITERAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ See my brother, a healer ◊
2→∿ Give kindness to deficiencies ∿
3→∿ Many failed under the burden ∿
4→In faith your hand is not withdrawn
5→∞ Sees falling and raises up ∞
6→Among the beasts I escaped
7→◊ In the conversation of my voice, lamentation ◊
8→∿ Love in truth will pursue ∿
9→∿ In listening, the heart will understand ∿
10→∞ Your name is miracle and redemption ∞

CONTEXTUAL TRANSLATION:
1→◊ Behold my brother, the wounded healer ◊
2→∿ Extend compassion to all lacks and wounds ∿
3→∿ Many have fallen under life's burdens ∿
4→In faith, your healing hand is never withdrawn
5→∞ Sees the fallen and lifts them up ∞
6→Among the wild forces I found refuge
7→◊ In the sharing of my voice, there is mourning ◊
8→∿ True love shall pursue all souls ∿
9→∿ Through deep listening, the heart shall understand ∿
10→∞ Your name embodies miracle and redemption ∞

THEMATIC INTERPRETATION:
This Chiron text speaks of the wounded healer who transforms personal suffering into compassionate service. It describes the one who extends healing to others' wounds and deficiencies, who lifts up the fallen, and whose own pain becomes a source of divine compassion. Chiron represents the principle of the wounded healer - the one who transforms personal wounds into wisdom and uses suffering as a gateway to divine service and redemption.
